# MicroAlchemy Announces $2.16M Pre-Seed Round

MicroAlchemy has raised a **$2.16 million pre-seed round** from [**Caffeinated Capital**](https://www.caffeinated.com/) and [**Haystack**](https://haystack.vc/).

With this MicroAlchemy will be building a proof of concept foundry and building out our EDA toolchain.
